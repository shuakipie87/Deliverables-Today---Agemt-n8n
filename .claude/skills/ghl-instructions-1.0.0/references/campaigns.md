## Email Campaigns

### Creating Email Campaigns
1. Go to **Marketing** > **Emails**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Choose template or start blank
4. Design your email
5. Set recipients and schedule
6. Send or schedule

### Email Builder

#### Drag-and-Drop Elements
- Text blocks
- Images
- Buttons
- Dividers
- Social links
- Video embeds

#### Personalization
Use merge fields for personalization:
- `{{contact.first_name}}` - First name
- `{{contact.email}}` - Email
- `{{contact.phone}}` - Phone
- `{{custom_field.field_name}}` - Custom fields

### Email Templates
1. Go to **Marketing** > **Templates**
2. Create reusable templates
3. Organize by category
4. Use in campaigns and workflows

### Email Best Practices
- Clear subject lines (under 50 chars)
- Single call-to-action
- Mobile-responsive design
- Test before sending
- Include unsubscribe link

## SMS Campaigns

### Creating SMS Campaigns
1. Go to **Marketing** > **SMS**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Write your message
4. Select recipients
5. Send or schedule

### SMS Guidelines
- Keep under 160 characters
- Include business name
- Provide opt-out option
- Respect quiet hours
- Use personalization

### SMS Templates
Save common messages:
- Appointment reminders
- Promotional offers
- Follow-up messages
- Thank you messages

### Compliance
- Get explicit consent
- Honor opt-out requests
- Follow TCPA regulations
- Maintain DNC lists

## Funnels

### Creating Funnels
1. Go to **Sites** > **Funnels**
2. Click **Create Funnel**
3. Choose template or blank
4. Build funnel steps
5. Connect pages
6. Publish

### Funnel Structure

#### Common Funnel Types
1. **Lead Magnet Funnel**
   - Landing page with opt-in
   - Thank you page
   - Delivery page

2. **Webinar Funnel**
   - Registration page
   - Confirmation page
   - Webinar room
   - Replay page

3. **Sales Funnel**
   - Sales page
   - Order form
   - Upsell pages
   - Thank you page

4. **Application Funnel**
   - Qualifier page
   - Application form
   - Confirmation page

### Funnel Elements
- Headlines and text
- Images and videos
- Forms and surveys
- Countdown timers
- Testimonials
- Pricing tables
- Order forms

### Funnel Settings
- Custom domain
- SEO settings
- Tracking codes
- Split testing
- Mobile optimization

## Marketing Automation

### Campaign Planning

#### Define Your Goals
- Lead generation
- Nurture sequences
- Sales conversion
- Customer retention
- Re-engagement

#### Map Customer Journey
1. Awareness (ads, content)
2. Interest (lead magnet)
3. Consideration (nurture)
4. Decision (offer)
5. Purchase (order)
6. Retention (follow-up)

### Automation Sequences

#### Welcome Sequence
Day 0: Welcome email
Day 1: Value content
Day 3: Case study
Day 5: Soft pitch
Day 7: Direct offer

#### Cart Abandonment
Hour 1: Reminder email
Day 1: Scarcity message
Day 3: Discount offer

#### Post-Purchase
Day 0: Thank you + receipt
Day 3: How-to guide
Day 7: Check-in
Day 14: Upsell offer
Day 30: Review request

### A/B Testing
- Test subject lines
- Test send times
- Test content variations
- Test CTAs
- Measure and optimize

### Tracking & Analytics

#### Key Metrics
- Open rates
- Click rates
- Conversion rates
- Unsubscribe rates
- Revenue per email

#### UTM Tracking
Add to all links:
- utm_source
- utm_medium
- utm_campaign
- utm_content

### Integration with Workflows
- Form submission triggers
- Tag-based automation
- Pipeline stage triggers
- Behavioral triggers
- Time-based sequences
